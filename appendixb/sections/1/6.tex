\exercise
An $n$-tuple of elements $a_1$, $a_2$, \dots, $a_n$ is defined as:
\[
    (a_1,a_2,\dots,a_n) =
    \begin{Cases}
        \emptyset & \text{if $n=0$}, \\
        \{a_1\} & \text{if $n=1$}, \\
        \{a_1,\{a_1,a_2\}\} & \text{if $n=2$}, \\
        (a_1,(a_2,\dots,a_n)) & \text{if $n\ge3$}.
    \end{Cases}
\]
