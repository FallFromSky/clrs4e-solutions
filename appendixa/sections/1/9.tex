\exercise
By formulas (A.7) and (A.11), we have:
\begin{align*}
    \sum_{k=0}^\infty\frac{k-1}{2^k} &= \sum_{k=0}^\infty\frac{k}{2^k} - \sum_{k=0}^\infty\frac{1}{2^k} \\
    &= \sum_{k=0}^\infty k(1/2)^k - \sum_{k=0}^\infty(1/2)^k \\
    &= \frac{1/2}{(1-1/2)^2} - \frac{1}{1-1/2} \\
    &= 0.
\end{align*}
