Asymptotic upper bound:
\begin{align*}
    \sum_{k=1}^n k^r &\le \sum_{k=1}^n n^r \\
    &= n\cdot n^r \\
    &= O(n^{r+1}).
\end{align*}
Asymptotic lower bound:
\begin{align*}
    \sum_{k=1}^n k^r &\ge \sum_{k=n/2+1}^n k^r \\
    &\ge \sum_{k=n/2+1}^n (n/2)^r \\
    &= (n/2)\cdot (n/2)^r \\
    &= \Omega(n^{r+1}).
\end{align*}
Based on the results, we conclude that the asymptotic tight bound on the summation is
\[
    \sum_{k=1}^n k^r = \Theta(n^{r+1}).
\]
