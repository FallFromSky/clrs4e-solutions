Since $(A\cap B)\cup(\overline{A}\cap B)=B$, and since events $A\cap B$ and $\overline{A}\cap B$ are mutually exclusive, we get
\begin{align*}
    \Pr{A\mid B}+\Pr{\overline{A}\mid B} &= \frac{\Pr{A\cap B}}{\Pr{B}}+\frac{\Pr{\overline{A}\cap B}}{\Pr{B}} \\[1mm]
    &= \frac{\Pr{B}}{\Pr{B}} \\
    &= 1.
\end{align*}
