For $n=0$ the equality holds trivially, so let $n\ge1$.
Then
\begin{align*}
    \sum_{k=0}^n\binom{n}{k}k &= 0+\sum_{k=1}^n\binom{n-1}{k-1}n && \text{(by identity (C.9))} \\
    &= n\sum_{k=0}^{n-1}\binom{n-1}{k} \\[1mm]
    &= n2^{n-1} && \text{(by indentity (C.4) where $x=y=1$)}.
\end{align*}
